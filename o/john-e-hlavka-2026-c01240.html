In Memoriam

John E. Hlavka

1950 – 2026

Funeral services will be held at Trinity Lutheran Church (2065 Geneva Street Racine 53402) on Tuesday, June 23, 2026 at 11:00 am with Pastor Stephen Jennings officiating. Interment will follow in Mound Cemetery. Visitation will be in the church on Tuesday from 9:30-11:00 am.

In memory of John, memorials to Trinity Lutheran Church or to the charity of one’s choice have been suggested.

John Edward Hlavka, age 76, passed away peacefully, surrounded with the unending love of family, Tuesday morning, June 16, 2026.

John was a native of Racine born on January 20, 1950 to the late John and Fern (nee: Ayers) Hlavka. He graduated from Racine Lutheran High School in 1968. After attending UW-Parkside, John began a 38-year career with InSinkErator, retiring as a supervisor in 2011.

On January 2, 1971, John was united in marriage to his high school sweetheart, Carol Kalb. John & Carol are longtime members of Trinity Lutheran Church. John was a sports enthusiast with a special affinity for the Green Bay Packers. He enjoyed playing city league sports including softball, basketball, volleyball and bowling. In his later years he shifted from being a participant to devoted spectator rarely missing his children’s and grandchildren’s sporting events. In quiet times, he enjoyed model ship building, Sci-Fi (especially Dune), and reading.

Surviving are his loving wife, Carol Hlavka; daughter, Kristine (Rick) Hopf; son, John (Katie) Hlavka; grandchildren, Sienna Hopf, Wyatt Hopf, Mia Hlavka, Grace Kim, Benjamin Kim and Max Kim; brothers & sisters, James Hlavka, Carol Wiechers, Lee (Zsuzsa) Hlavka, Rick Hlavka, Evelyn (Louis) Schneider, Kevin (Linda) Hlavka, Brian (Julie) Hlavka and Lynn (Rich) Zmijewski; sister-in-law, Jan Wolfgram; brother-I n-law, James (Anne) Kalb; many nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends – too numerous to mention all by name.

In addition to his parents, John was preceded in death by his sisters, Judith Heather and Georgia Hlavka; sisters-in-law, Judith Hlavka and Sandra Hlavka; brothers-in-law, James Heather, Roger Wolfgram and James Wiechers; and by Carol’s parents, Gustave and Hildegarde (nee: Nommensen) Kalb.

Funeral services will be held at Trinity Lutheran Church (2065 Geneva Street Racine 53402) on Tuesday, June 23, 2026 at 11:00 am with Pastor Stephen Jennings officiating. Interment will follow in Mound Cemetery. Visitation will be in the church on Tuesday from 9:30-11:00 am. In memory of John, memorials to Trinity Lutheran Church or to the charity of one’s choice have been suggested.

A special thank you is extended to Dr. Howard Short, who was his cardiologist for more than 45 years! John had the distinction of being Dr. Short’s longest-tenured patient when he retired, reflecting a remarkable physician-patient relationship that lasted nearly half a century. Many thanks to Brighton Hospice, especially Angie, for the compassionate care and support given in John’s time of need. May God bless all of you!
