In Memoriam

Cynthia Louise Nicholson

1951 – 2026

Cynthia Louise Nicholson, 74, of Racine, died Saturday, June 6, 2026, at her residence.

Cynthia Louise Nicholson’s Early Life

She was born Nov. 23, 1951, in Racine to the late Earl and Jeanette (Lockhart) King. Cindy graduated from Washington Park High School.

She was united in marriage to John Nicholson in El Paso, Texas, on Oct. 4, 1972. Cindy was a longtime member of First Evangelical Lutheran Church . She enjoyed reading and collecting giraffes. Most of all, she loved her dogs.

Survived by

Cindy is survived by her daughter, Emily (William Chaney Jr.) Nicholson; her sister, Stephanie King; and nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.

She was preceded in death by her husband, John Nicholson, who died May 2, 2022, and her sister, Pamela Kafka.

Services

A funeral service will be held at 11 a.m. Monday, June 15, 2026, at First Evangelical Lutheran Church, 728 Villa St. Visitation will be held from 10 a.m. until the time of service Monday at the church. Cindy will be laid to rest at West Lawn Memorial Park
