In Memoriam

Lori J. Barnes

1958 – 2026

Celebration of life will take place on what would have been her 68th birthday on July 10, 2026. The celebration will take place at Reese Events, 1962 Taylor Ave Racine, Wisconsin from 4pm-8pm.

Colors are teal white and sliver, but not mandatory .

Lori J Barnes passed away unexpectedly in Milwaukee, Wisconsin on June 16, 2026. Lori was born in Racine, Wisconsin on July 10, 1958, to Harry & Geraldine Drechsler. She graduated from Case High School and later furthered her education at the University of Wisconsin Parkside.

She worked at Western Publishing for several years. She retired in 2023 from Chrysler Parts plant in Milwaukee.

Lori leaves to cherish the love of her life for over 45 years James E Smith; children, Daodie (Carletta) Moore and Michelle Jeanna Barnes; six grandchildren, Carletta Yesenia, JuQuan, Quintereus (Emily), Quinaysha, and Tar’Neeja, (Lori’s best friend); 10 great grandchildren; sisters, Debra (Thomas) Pate and Lynn (John) Parker.

She is proceeded in death by her parents.

Per her wishes, no services will be held. However, a celebration of life will take place on what would have been her 68th birthday on July 10, 2026. The celebration will take place at Reese Events, 1962 Taylor Ave Racine, Wisconsin from 4pm-8pm. Please come out and celebrate Lori’s life and special day. Colors are teal white and sliver, but not mandatory.
